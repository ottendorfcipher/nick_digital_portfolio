Private Cello Lessons

I currently teach private lessons at Beechland Baptist Church to kids and adults of all ages in Louisville, KY. If you are interested in taking private cello lessons, please use this contact form to get in touch with me.

I typically use a combination of the Sassmanhaus and Suzuki method for young beginners. Then I follow the American String Teachers Association's cello repetoire list to select appropriate methods, etudes and pieces for cello students. At the end of each term, students have the option to perform in a recital. I love working with private students and joyfully teach students of all levels and abilities!
