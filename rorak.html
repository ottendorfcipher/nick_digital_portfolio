About Rorak

My cousin introduced me to forums when he and I were teenagers. I felt an immediate affinity toward forums like Neopets and Gaia Online. In 2007, I met Lawren Barnett in an online clan and we became close friends. Several years later, Lawren and I discussed creating our own forum hangout game based on the idea of a fantasy world he had developed over several years. At the time though, neither of us had experience with web development or had significant artistic skills. Despite this, we were extraordinarily passionate about developing an immersive fantasy world and online community.

In 2017, I reached out to Andrew Rodrigues, the co-founder of an innovative forum company - NodeBB. We spoke about our idea and it turned out that creating an engine for this type of hangout space was something that Andrew was equally passionate about. He mentioned that the only bottleneck experienced in the past was finding a talented artist who could create avatar art and various assets. Soon after Andrew and I's inital conversation, I reached out to YinIchigo, an incredibly talented avatar artist who offered to be a part of our team and we then began development to realize this collective vision.

Last year in 2020, Lawren, Andrew and I incorporated Rorak LLC and Andrew began a new company called OpenFantasy Inc. based in Toronto, Canada to serve as the game engine for the world of Rorak. Rorak's long-term vision is to develop and facilitate an incredible forum hangout community.
