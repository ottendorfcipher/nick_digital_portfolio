In February 2021, I was blessed with the opportunity to conduct a small ensemble of young string musicians after school at Beechland Academy of Music, located at Beechland Baptist Church in Southwest Louisville. We had a wonderful socially-distanced, masked in-person concert in May and I'm looking forward to the future of this community youth orchestra.

If you are interested in having your child participate in this community youth orchestra, please use the contact form to reach out. Students who are enrolled in private lessons with a strings teacher at the academy recieve a discount to participate.
